About LiveStock



LiveStock is a portable device paired with a mobile app that allows smallholder farmers to accurately and affordably measure the weight of their pigs without costly scales or stressful manual methods. Powered by AI and IoT technology, it provides instant, reliable results even offline, helping farmers avoid financial losses from inaccurate estimates, improve livestock management, and secure fair income for their hard work.
